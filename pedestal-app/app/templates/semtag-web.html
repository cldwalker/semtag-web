First time here? Learn more about semtag.

Semtag is a site that stores bookmarks as things/entities. Bookmarks and their tags are treated equally as things with a type. Each thing can have associations (tags) with other things a.k.a. semantic tagging. For example, here is my bookmark for google and here are all the things tagged with it. If you clicked on google, you see that it's a company type. To see what other types I track, see the types page.
